# Prepare Discovery Session Materials

When this skill is invoked, prepare everything Juan Jose needs to run a discovery session with the MNL team.

## Step 1: Determine session scope

Ask Juan Jose (or infer from context):
- Which dimension(s) are we discovering? (Estrategia, Operacion, Contenido, Reporting, Cultura)
- Which campaign phases are relevant? (1-9)
- Who will attend? (CMs, Strategy, Comercial, Carlos, specific names)
- Is this a first discovery or a follow-up?

## Step 2: Read existing context

Read these files to avoid re-asking what we already know:
- `clients/mamba-negra/knowledge/campaign-framework.md` — current process understanding
- `clients/mamba-negra/strategy/PROCESS-AI-MAP.md` — known pain points and opportunities
- `clients/mamba-negra/FASE1A-DISCOVERY.md` — any previous discovery notes
- `clients/mamba-negra/knowledge/verticals/` — vertical-specific context

## Step 3: Generate session materials

Use `my-toolkit/templates/discovery-session-guide.md` as the base and customize:

### A) Agenda (filled, not blank)
Pre-fill the agenda with:
- Specific objective for THIS session
- Time blocks adapted to scope (30 min for focused, 60 min for broad)
- Named participants

### B) Role-specific questions

Generate targeted questions based on attendees:

**For CMs (Campaign Managers)**:
- Focus on: scouting process, Modash usage, content approval flow, time spent on repetitive tasks
- Ask about: specific campaigns they found difficult, tools they wish they had
- Probe: how they currently find influencers, vet them, track deliverables

**For Strategy**:
- Focus on: brief interpretation, proposal building, KPI definition, competitive analysis
- Ask about: information gaps when building strategy, what they wish they knew about the audience
- Probe: how vertical-specific decisions are made

**For Comercial**:
- Focus on: client expectations, reporting needs, what they promise vs what gets delivered
- Ask about: client feedback patterns, most requested improvements
- Probe: how they communicate campaign status to clients

**For Carlos (Director)**:
- Focus on: business priorities, vision for AI, budget constraints, competitive pressure
- Ask about: what keeps him up at night, what would a "10x improvement" look like
- Probe: how he evaluates ROI, what success looks like in 3/6/12 months

### C) Data collection template

Create a structured note-taking template with sections:
- Proceso actual (como funciona hoy) — with specific sub-questions per phase
- Fricciones (donde duele) — ranked by severity
- Oportunidades de IA — mapped to specific agent/tool
- Vision del equipo — what they want, not what we think they need
- Datos cuantitativos — time estimates, frequencies, volumes

### D) Pre-session checklist

Generate a checklist for Juan Jose:
- [ ] Confirm attendance with participants (WhatsApp/email)
- [ ] Review previous discovery notes (if any)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 specific campaign examples to reference during discussion
- [ ] Have note-taking tool ready
- [ ] Test bots are working (if doing live demo)
- [ ] Block follow-up time (24h after session to process notes)

## Step 4: Save materials

Save the prepared session guide to a dated file:
`clients/mamba-negra/adoption/WORKSHOPS/discovery-[dimension]-[date].md`

## Step 5: Remind

Tell Juan Jose:
- Estimated session duration
- Key questions to NOT skip (the 3 most important)
- What to listen for (signals that reveal real pain vs stated pain)
- Post-session: process notes within 24h, update PROCESS-AI-MAP.md and knowledge/ files
